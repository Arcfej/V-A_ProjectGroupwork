Gemini
Zodiac Dates: May 21st - June 20th
One of the Zodiac constellations, Gemini is Latin for twins and represents the brothers Castor and Pollux of Greek mythology. They shared a mother however Castor was the son of the ‘King of Sparta’ while Pollux was the son of Zeus. When Castor was killed Zeus offered Pollux the choice to share his immortality with his brother and the two of them were cast into the sky.

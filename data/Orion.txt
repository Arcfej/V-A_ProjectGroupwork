Orion
Catalogued by Ptolemy in the 2nd Century
Orion is one of the most famous constellations in the sky. It is often depicted as the Greek hunter Orion facing off against the bull Taurus. Other times it shows Orion chasing a hare with his two hunting dogs represented by the constellation Canis Minor and Canis Major. The constellation was first mentioned by Greek author Homer in the 7th or 8th century BC.
